# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Lore to be a useful, welcoming project for people building local-first
developer tools. Be kind, direct, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different levels of experience.
- Give technical feedback without personal attacks.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections gracefully.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Threats, intimidation, or sustained disruption.
- Repeatedly pushing off-topic arguments after maintainers ask you to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may temporarily or permanently
ban contributors who violate this code of conduct.

Report concerns privately to the maintainer through GitHub or X / Twitter:
https://x.com/tana_shahh
